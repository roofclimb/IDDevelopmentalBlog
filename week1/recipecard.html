Banana cake recipe



Ingredients

Number Item Quantity
1. Ripe Banana 2
2. Butter (room temperature) 150 g
3. Sugar 150 g
4. Egg 2
5. Vanilla extract 1 tsp
6. Milk 100 ml
7. All-purpose flour 200 g
8. Baking powder 1tsp
9. Baking soda Â½ tsp

Method

  1. Take 2 ripe bananas in a bowl and smash it well.
  2. Add butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, milk and beat well until it combines.
  3. Add the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda and mix well.
  4. Preheat oven at 200 C.
  5. Pour the batter onto a greased cake tin and bake for 35 minutes.
  6. Remove the cake from the pan and let them cool completely.
  7. Enjoy this delicious moist and fluffy cake.
